What Is Included Inside DFY Video Agency?

The package includes three main software applications:

Vidictive

Vidictive provides access to more than 250 high-converting video ad templates designed for social media marketing campaigns.

Users can easily customize videos using a drag-and-drop editor and quickly create engaging promotional content.

Storie

Storie helps users create short-form video content suitable for platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and other social networks.

The software focuses on producing visually engaging content that attracts attention and increases engagement.

Videlligence

Videlligence uses artificial intelligence technology to transform product pages into ready-to-use video advertisements.

Simply paste a product link from platforms like Shopify, Amazon, eBay, or Etsy, and the software automatically extracts product details, images, and pricing information to generate a professional marketing video.
